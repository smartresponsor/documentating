= Paying
:description: Public entry page for the Paying component.
:page-slug: paying

Paying is a Symfony-oriented payment lifecycle, webhook, and payment-operations component within the Smartresponsor ecosystem.

It should be understood not as a simple gateway adapter, but as a governed payment layer for create, start, finalize, refund, and read flows, webhook ingest and verification, idempotent payment operations, operator-console runtime, and long-horizon payment discipline.

== What It Is

Paying exists to provide a structured internal payment capability that can support payment lifecycle orchestration, canonical API/UI/CLI surfaces, webhook ingest, idempotency, outbox publishing, health and metrics exposure, provider routing, and governed operational verification.

In ecosystem terms, it belongs to the class of components that strengthen payment correctness, operational trust, and revenue-event continuity.

== Current Reading

In its current state, Paying already reads as a serious pre-RC component.

Its strongest visible qualities are:

* clear payment-lifecycle product identity,
* strong webhook, idempotency, and provider-routing posture,
* multi-surface runtime with API, UI, and CLI layers,
* and a governance-oriented engineering contour around proofs, smokes, reports, and security checks.

== Why It Matters

Payment systems become fragile when lifecycle state, refunds, provider integration, webhook trust, idempotency, outbox delivery, health visibility, and operator review surfaces are treated as separate ad hoc concerns.

Paying matters because it treats those concerns as part of one governed component surface rather than as scattered utility logic.

That makes it strategically more valuable than a simple payment-form backend or a thin provider SDK wrapper.
