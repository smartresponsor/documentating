= Vendoring
:description: Public entry page for the Vendoring component.
:page-slug: vendoring

Vendoring is a Symfony-oriented vendor operations, transaction, statement, and payout component within the Smartresponsor ecosystem.

It should be understood not as a simple vendor directory or supplier CRUD layer, but as a governed vendor-facing operational component for transaction runtime slices, statements, payouts, persistence contracts, and release-candidate quality discipline.

== What It Is

Vendoring exists to provide a structured internal vendor capability that can support vendor transactions, payout-related operations, statement delivery, persistence integrity, and governed operational handling of vendor-facing flows.

In ecosystem terms, it belongs to the class of components that strengthen supplier operations, vendor transaction discipline, and operational trust around vendor runtime.

== Current Reading

In its current state, Vendoring already reads as a serious pre-RC component.

Its strongest visible qualities are:

* clear vendor-operations product identity,
* unusually strong release-candidate quality contour,
* transaction-aware and payout-aware internal modeling,
* and a governance-oriented engineering posture around vendor runtime.

== Why It Matters

Vendor systems become fragmented when supplier records, transactions, statements, payouts, contracts, and runtime governance are treated as separate ad hoc concerns.

Vendoring matters because it treats those concerns as part of one governed component surface rather than as scattered utility logic.

That makes it strategically more valuable than a simple supplier list or payment helper.
