= Cataloging
:description: Public entry page for the Cataloging component.
:page-slug: cataloging

Cataloging is a Symfony-oriented catalog, category, and taxonomy governance component within the Smartresponsor ecosystem.

It should be understood not as a simple category tree helper, but as a governed catalog and classification layer for category operations, product navigation, structured catalog surfaces, and long-horizon taxonomy discipline.

== What It Is

Cataloging exists to provide a structured internal catalog capability that can support category modeling, catalog navigation, taxonomy handling, route-aware runtime access, and governed evolution of classification surfaces.

In ecosystem terms, it belongs to the class of components that strengthen catalog structure, product discoverability, and operational category governance.

== Current Reading

In its current state, Cataloging already reads as a serious pre-RC component.

Its strongest visible qualities are:

* clear catalog-and-category product identity,
* strong QA and runtime verification culture,
* structured smoke and inspection contour,
* and a governance-oriented engineering posture around taxonomy and catalog runtime.

== Why It Matters

Catalog systems become fragmented when category structure, routing, discoverability, product navigation, and operational catalog governance are treated as separate ad hoc concerns.

Cataloging matters because it treats those concerns as part of one governed component surface rather than as scattered utility logic.

That makes it strategically more valuable than a simple category CRUD layer or storefront menu helper.
